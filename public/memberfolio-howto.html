Before you start

What you'll need

A few simple things — no special equipment.

Good to know: New registrations are reviewed and approved by the cooperative before your first login. That waiting step is completely normal.
  • A valid email address you can open today
  • A Philippine mobile number (09XXXXXXXXX)
  • About 5 minutes of your time
  • If you're already a member — your Membership Number

1STEP 1

Start at the ISATUCMPC website

Open isatucmpc.coop in your browser, then click “Member Login” at the top-right.

The Member Login button is also in the footer. Clicking it opens the member portal at app.isatucmpc.coop.

7STEP 7 · IF PROMPTED

Enter your 6-digit code

If your account uses 2-step verification, you'll see “Two-Factor Verification.” Enter the 6-digit code sent to your email.

Different from Step 4. The email link confirmed your address. This 6-digit code simply signs you in. The code expires in 10 minutes.
