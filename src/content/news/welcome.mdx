After more than six decades of serving the Iloilo community, ISATUCMPC has opened a new door — our first website at **isatucmpc.coop**.

This site is for everyone touched by the cooperative:

- **Members** — to find services, news, governance updates, and member resources.
- **Prospective members** — to learn how to join, what benefits await, and how the cooperative works.
- **Our wider community** — partners, regulators, and friends who want to understand who we are.

The site joins our [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/isatu.cmpc) and the soon-to-launch MemberFolio portal as the three official channels for ISATUCMPC online.

We'll keep adding more content over time — per-service deep pages, financial reports, the PMES schedule, and a resources hub. If something you're looking for isn't here yet, please [contact us](/contact).

**Mag-uswag kita upod.** Let's grow together.
